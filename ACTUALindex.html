Why was Jenny Wearing Red Pants?

Sweatpants

Jenny's red pants were not what many upper-class members would classify as pants, rather, they were sweatpants. Jenny loved her sweatpants. She had many variations and colors, but the red were her favorite. They were red Air Jordan sweatpants her mom had bought her from the boys section of Gabe's. Jenny was in fact a tomboy. She grew up playing outside and inside. Inside, she played basketball. Outside, she also played basketball in addition to softball, wiffleball, kickball, football, etc. The one sport she took interest in that didn't end in "ball" was frisbee. Her sweatpants came in handy, you see, as she sweat a lot. She felt as though they were made for her: comfortable, easy to play in, and they looked cool. They also made spinning on cardboard easy when she performed a breakdancing routine in her school's talent show. She was the first act. Her dad came late from work and missed it. She didn't care, but he did. Her favorite color was not red, but green, so her affection toward her red pants was just as much of a surprise to her as it was to anyone else. What could she get away with in the red pants? Many things, but spilling red paint on her pants in school without anyone realizing should not go unnoticed.
